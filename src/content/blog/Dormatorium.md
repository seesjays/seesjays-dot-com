In approximately 9-ish hours, I’ll be moving into my dorm.

I am uncertain of the existences, mannerisms, or personalities of my roommates, as I was randomly assigned. Hope they’re kind.

To be frank, I feel none of the "certain dread" or "nervousness" of leaving home, because I’ll actually be somewhat close. Houston isn’t far away from my primary abode, so I could visit often. I’m excited for that, at least.

I’m actually in the process of packing. I don’t have much to bring, really. My room at home is quite plain (as that’s how I like it) and thus shall be my dorm. Maybe I’ll hang up my vinyls, but nothing more extravagant than that.

Thinking further into it, I’m actually quite uncertain about everything. Such is the nature of new experiences.
