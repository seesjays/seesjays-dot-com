I think I’ve selected a really good set of classes this semester. That is, the stuff I have looks really _engaging!_

A good half hour was spent perusing through my Programming and Data Structures (which I can take now, since CB finally uploaded my AP scores!) syllabus, and I felt a genuine sense of excitement. "Oh, all the stuff I should probably know but don’t!" It’s marvelous, really.

Other than the neat looking CS course, I’m also in Calc 2, which is great because I love Calculus as well. The thing I’m looking forward to less, however, is my Geology class+lab. I do not care for rocks, but I care much less for the other options, other options being Physics, Chemistry, or Biology. Geology just seemed like the easiest of the three to me.

To top it all off, I have a virtual Technical Communications class on Monday evenings. Lovely, now I’ll learn to write better documentation.
